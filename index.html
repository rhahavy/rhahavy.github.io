01Approach

Where policy meets data.

My analytical work is shaped by a Political Science foundation — I'm as interested in the systems a dataset describes as in the numbers themselves. Housing markets, education outcomes, customer behavior: each one is a human system with stakes beyond the spreadsheet.

That perspective shows up in how I build. Every dashboard is designed around a decision — what the number means, who needs to see it, and what should happen next. The goal isn't more charts. It's less ambiguity.

04Selected Work

Case studies.

Each project starts with a business question and ends with a visual, decision-ready answer.

Real Estate 01 / 03

GTA Housing Market Analysis

Where has affordability in the GTA slipped fastest — and what's behind it?

Long-run analysis of prices, inventory, and affordability across Greater Toronto municipalities. Built to pinpoint when and where the market turned — and which regions have diverged most from the provincial trend.

Impact: surfaces 26 years of price-vs-payment pressure in one view.

Customer Analytics 02 / 03

Telecom Customer Churn Dashboard

Which customers are most at risk of leaving, and where should retention spend go?

Segmented a telecom subscriber base by tenure, service mix, and payment behavior to surface the cohorts most likely to churn — and the levers that would have the highest retention payoff.

Impact: identifies the customer cohorts worth retaining first.

Education 03 / 03

Ontario EQAO Dashboard

How has student performance shifted across Ontario regions and school systems?

Benchmarked board-level EQAO outcomes against the provincial average to surface multi-year trends and the widest post-pandemic gaps — with filters designed for program and policy teams.

Impact: highlights the boards with the largest learning recovery gaps.
